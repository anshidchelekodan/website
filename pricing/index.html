Pricing FAQ

Common questions about my engagement model and billing.

Do you have fixed monthly packages?

I prefer a project-based approach because no two businesses are the same. However, for ongoing retainers like SEO or Ad Management, I create a customized monthly flat fee based on the agreed-upon scope of work and ad spend volume.

Do you charge a percentage of Ad Spend?

For Performance Marketing (Google/Meta Ads), my pricing typically involves a base management fee plus a small performance percentage on ad spend over a certain threshold. This ensures my goals align with your ROI.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. My project-based approach guarantees complete transparency. You will know exactly what the costs are, what deliverables are included, and the expected timelines before we sign any agreements.
