SJS Business Solutions Case Study

Lead Generation Kerala SME Marketing Malappuram, Kerala

How I helped a business consulting firm generate high-quality leads using a specialized Meta Ads to WhatsApp funnel.

Project Overview

SJS Business Solutions is a professional business consulting and operations management company. They focus on improving business efficiency, structuring complex operations, and building scalable systems for growth. As an independent Digital Marketing Strategist in Malappuram, I was tasked with building their digital lead acquisition engine.

My Role & Contribution

I joined as a Digital Marketing & Lead Generation Partner, taking full responsibility for the campaign's success. My goal was to move beyond simple brand awareness and focus strictly on high-intent lead acquisition from business owners and SMEs in Kerala.

Strategy & Execution

The strategy was built on three pillars: precise targeting, friction-less communication, and performance-based optimization.

  • Meta Ads Mastery (Facebook & Instagram)

    Developed hyper-targeted ad strategies specifically designed to reach business owners and decision-makers across Kerala who were actively seeking growth solutions.

  • WhatsApp Funnel Integration

    Instead of complex forms, we integrated WhatsApp Business as the primary conversion point. This direct communication line significantly increased the trust factor and lead-to-client conversion rate.

  • Creative Optimization

    Designed ad creatives and messaging that resonated with the pain points of Kerala-based entrepreneurs, ensuring the highest possible CTR (Click-Through Rate).
