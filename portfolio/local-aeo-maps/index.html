Local Brand Domination via AEO

Local Maps & AEO 4 Months Duration

The Challenge

A prominent wellness clinic in Malappuram was struggling to get foot traffic from digital channels. People frequently searched for "wellness clinics near me" or used voice assistants to ask for services, but traditional SEO Optimization wasn't enough to secure the Local 3-Pack spots.

Our Strategy

We pivoted to a hyper-local approach combined with Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), structuring the online presence to explicitly and directly answer questions via Google SGE (Search Generative Experience) and Voice Search.

  • Answer Engine Content Structuring

    Rebuilt service pages with FAQ blocks optimized for AI-chat answers. We created targeted response content matching queries like "What is the best wellness treatment in Malappuram?"

  • Google Business Profile Hyper-Optimization

    Uploaded weekly hyper-local geo-tagged images, responded to all reviews using NLP intent keywords, and aggressively updated Google Post offers.

  • Local Citation Network & PR

    Synchronized NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data across 50+ Kerala local directories and secured features in local news portals to build immense geographical authority.

300+ Map Direction Requests
#1 Local 3-Pack Rank

Key Takeaway

"Local businesses don't just need website traffic; they need foot traffic. Optimizing for AEO and Local Maps is the fastest bridge between a digital search and a physical visit."
