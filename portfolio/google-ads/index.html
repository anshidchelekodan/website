Google Ads Lead Generation

Performance Marketing High-Growth Startup

Project Overview

A fast-growing tech startup needed to scale their lead generation quickly while keeping acquisition costs under control. Their existing self-managed campaigns were suffering from broad matching issues and high bounce rates, leading to a waste of ad budget. They reached out for expert Performance Marketing intervention.

The Solution

We completely restructured their Google Ads account, shifting from loose targeting to a high-intent, funnel-based architecture.

  • Hyper-Targeted Keyword Selection

    Replaced broad generic keywords with high-intent long-tail phrases and implemented a robust negative keyword list to eliminate irrelevant traffic.

  • Landing Page Optimization

    Designed dedicated, fast-loading landing pages with clear CTAs and trust signals, significantly improving the Quality Score and conversion rates.

  • A/B Testing & Bid Mastery

    Ran continuous A/B tests on ad copy and implemented automated bidding strategies to maximize conversions within the target CPA range.

-40% Cost Per Acquisition
3x Lead Volume Increase

The Result

"By aligning the ad message perfectly with a high-conversion landing page, we turned a leaking budget into a high-performance growth engine."
