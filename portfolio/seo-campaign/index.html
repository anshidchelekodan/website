SEO Campaign – Local Business

SEO Optimization 6 Months Duration

The Challenge

A leading local service provider in Malappuram was struggling with low search visibility. Despite having a quality offering, their website was buried on the 3rd and 4th pages of Google, missing out on thousands of potential customers searching for their services locally. This required a specialized SEO Optimization approach.

Our Strategy

We implemented a multi-faceted SEO approach focused on technical hygiene, local relevance, and authoritative content, adhering to the latest Google SEO Fundamentals.

  • Technical Audit & Optimization

    We resolved critical indexing issues, improved site loading speeds by 60%, and implemented advanced Schema markup for better rich snippet visibility.

  • Local SEO Dominance

    Optimized the Google Business Profile, managed local citations, and created city-specific landing pages to capture "near me" searches in Malappuram.

  • Content & Link Building

    Developed a high-impact content schedule targeting long-tail keywords and secured quality backlinks from reputable local and industry sources.
