E-Commerce SEO Scaling

Technical SEO 8 Months Duration

The Challenge

A growing e-commerce brand based in Kerala had thousands of products but minimal organic traffic. They were losing out to large marketplaces like Amazon and Flipkart for valuable long-tail product searches. They needed a robust SEO Optimization overhaul tailored specifically for online retail.

Our Strategy

We executed a highly technical, product-focused strategy designed to capture high-intent buyers exactly when they were ready to purchase, turning the website into an organic sales funnel.

  • Taxonomy and Site Architecture

    Completely restructured category and sub-category hierarchies. Implemented logical URL patterns, making product discovery easier for both users and Google's crawl bots.

  • Advanced Schema Markup Implementation

    Injected comprehensive Product, Review, and Organization JSON-LD markup. This earned them highly-visible search results with star ratings and pricing directly in Google searches.

  • Product Description Optimization

    Rewrote hundreds of manufacturer-copy product descriptions. Implemented keyword-rich, unique content to resolve thin and duplicate content issues across the site.

+180% Organic Revenue
15k+ New Monthly Visitors

Key Takeaway

"In E-Commerce SEO, technical perfection and structured data are the most reliable ways to outrank massive marketplace competitors."
